This app is designed for the NFL historians. Using fantasydata's api, historical team statistics are presented in an elegant, and highly customizable format. It gives users the ability to present data spanning from this years Super Bowl Winning Denver Broncos, all the way back to the historical 1985 Bears and the teams that were left in their wake.

Now try it below! Just select the year you want to see data for. Select offensive stats will be displayed by default, but you can also seperate by three additional categories. These include defensive, special teams and situational. You can change the data on the next page to suit your needs. From there you can sort teams by ranking and view their team pages with a seasonal report card breaking down their performance.
